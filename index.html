About Me

My research interests lie in AI explainability, reasoning, reinforcement learning, planning under uncertainty and robotics, with a strong emphasis on human-in-the-loop systems. I'm interested in solving problems around understanding black-box AI agents(Sequential Decision-Making Systems), combining planning and learning, action model learning, hierarchical planning, reward shaping,POMDPs and more

I am seeking PhD positions in leading labs that specialize in these areas. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from BITS Pilani Hyderabad, India and a Master’s degree from Arizona State University, where I gained valuable research experience in the AAIR lab. Additionally, I have over two years of industry experience. My goal is to solve world problems,contribute and be of service to the scientific community.
